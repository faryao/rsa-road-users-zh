# Children - FAQs

### Peace of mind that your child's car seat is fitted correctly

According to our [Check it Fits research](http://www.rsa.ie/docs/default-source/about/frightening-statistic-that-shows-that-as-many-as-4-out-of-5-child-car-seats-are-incorrectly-fitted7188eb02-83e0-4ba1-9de2-fd7f8b50361a.pdf?sfvrsn=e20cc2bc_3), as many as 4 out of 5 child car seats are incorrectly fitted which can result in serious injury or a fatality in the event of a collision.

![Image 5: RSA Check it fits service](http://www.rsa.ie/images/default-source/in-page-full-width-(868-x-487)/check-it-fits.jpg?sfvrsn=7963f9f2_0)
Our 'Check it Fits' roadshow offers a full-time, nationwide, free service. Our team of experts will not only check that your child's car seat is fitted safely, they will also give a demonstration and answer any questions you might have.

A list of our upcoming 'Check it Fits' event locations and dates are listed on the map below. You don't need to book an appointment.
