# Vehicle components

### Vehicle components and legislation in Ireland.

Important safety standards for vehicle components such as lighting, seatbelts, vehicle glass, tyres, frontal protection systems and engine technology.

[Vehicle lighting Regulations on colours of vehicle lights, including fog lights, parking lamps and side-marker lamps Learn more](http://www.rsa.ie/road-safety/road-users/vehicle-components/vehicle-lighting)

[Seatbelts and child restraints The laws that apply, two-point and three-point seat belts, seat belt devices, safety checks for seat belts. Learn more](http://www.rsa.ie/road-safety/road-users/vehicle-components/vehicle-components/seatbelts-and-child-restraints)

[Vehicle glass Laws and safety standards for vehicle glass, window tinting, and line of vision. Learn more](http://www.rsa.ie/road-safety/road-users/vehicle-components/vehicle-glass)

[Tyres Tyres are the only part of your vehicle in contact with the road surface. That’s why it's vital you choose the appropriate tyres and keep them correctly maintained. Learn more](http://www.rsa.ie/road-safety/road-users/vehicle-components/tyres)

[Frontal protection systems (Bull bars) Laws on frontal protection systems such as bull bars, type-approved bull bars, and retrofitting bull bars to the front of a vehicle. Learn more](http://www.rsa.ie/road-safety/road-users/vehicle-components/frontal-protection-systems-(bull-bars))

[AdBlue or diesel exhaust fluid Engine system technologies designed to reduce harmful emissions, AdBlue, diesel exhaust fluid and Diesel particulate filters (DPF) Learn more](http://www.rsa.ie/road-safety/road-users/vehicle-components/adblue-or-diesel-exhaust-fluid)
