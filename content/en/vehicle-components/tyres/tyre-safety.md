# Tyre safety

### Tyres are the only part of your vehicle in contact with the road surface.

That’s why it's vital you choose the appropriate tyres and keep them correctly maintained.Your safety, and the safety of your passengers and other road users, depend on it.
This content is for general information only. It does not, and is not intended to, provide legal or technical advice or to represent a legal interpretation of the matters it addresses

## All of your tyres must:

*   meet the correct standard and size for your vehicle
*   be properly inflated
*   be free from defects
*   have a tread depth of at least 1.6mm
