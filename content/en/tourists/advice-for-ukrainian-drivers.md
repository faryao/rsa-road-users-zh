# Advice for Ukrainian drivers

### **What you need to know about driving in Ireland.****Що вам потрібно знати про водіння в Ірландії.**Что нужно знать о вождении в Ирландии.

EU legislation which came into effect on 27 July 2022 provides that people fleeing Ukraine and living in Ireland under temporary protection can drive on a valid Ukrainian driving licence without needing to exchange it for an Irish driving licence.

However, these temporary protections will end on 04 March 2027.

There is not currently a driving licence exchange agreement between Ireland and the Ukraine.

If you wish to continue to drive in Ireland after this date we would advise you will be required to follow the learner to licence process, before 04 March 2027. This involves passing a driver theory test, obtain an Irish learner permit, complete mandatory driving lessons (EDT), hold your learner permit for a period of 6 months and then pass a driving test in the B category (car).

Reduced Essential Driver Training is available to holders of a driving licence where Ireland does not have a recognised exchange agreement in place. For more details, please see our [Reduced EDT page.](http://www.rsa.ie/services/learner-drivers/driving-lessons/car-driving-lessons-(edt)/reduced-edt)

Professional truck and bus drivers with qualifications obtained in Ukraine, will be temporarily recognised in Ireland while holding a temporary residency status, following a training course and any checks, as required.

Where you still hold temporary protection status you may apply for a learner permit in the truck or bus category before 04 March 2027 by registering your Ukraine driving licence in the category B, and by first passing a driver theory test in the applicable category.

On passing a driving test in the applicable category, then apply for a driving licence in the applicable category where you will also obtain a driving licence in the car category with the same start date.

Please note: While holding this learner permit you can continue to drive on your Ukraine driving licence

### Road Safety in Ireland.Користування дорогами в Ірландії.Использование дорог в Ирландии.

There are almost 100,000 kilometres of roads in Ireland. These roads range from Motorway to narrow single lane rural roads.. Whether walking, cycling, motorcycling or driving, this guide will provide you with some basic rules of the road to ensure you can stay safe.

В Ірландії налічується майже 100 000 кілометрів доріг. Це як автомагістралі, так і вузькі односмугові сільські дороги. Цей посібник містить базові правила дорожнього руху для вашої безпеки під час ходіння, їзди на велосипеді, мотоциклі або автомобільному транспорті.

В Ирландии насчитывается почти 100 000 километров дорог. Это как автомагистрали, так и узкие сельские дороги. В данном руководстве содержатся правила дорожного движения для вашей безопасности во время ходьбы, езды на велосипеде, мотоцикле или автомобильном транспорте.
