# Introduction

### Important standards for agricultural vehicles such as tractors and low loaders.

If you regularly use your agricultural tractor for your work, e.g. farming, fisheries, forestry or horticulture, then there are important standards your vehicle must meet when using it on Irish roads.![Image 6](http://www.rsa.ie/images/default-source/in-page-full-width-(868-x-487)/red_tractor.jpg?Status=Master&sfvrsn=ba5bdbc4_3)

## Weights and dimensions for agricultural vehicles

From a road safety perspective and to protect Ireland’s road and bridges it is very important that all vehicles stay within their [legal limits for weights and dimensions](http://www.rsa.ie/road-safety/road-users/professional-drivers/vehicle-safety-legislation/weights-and-dimensions).

## Fast tractor testing

If your tractor has a maximum design speed exceeding 40 km/h and is used commercially, it may need to undergo a periodic [roadworthiness test Open in new browser tab](https://www.cvrt.ie/en-US/About-CVRT/Fast-Tractor-Testing/) and display a certificate of roadworthiness (CRW).
