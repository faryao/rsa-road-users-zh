# Seatbelts

### Law on use of seat belts in tractors.

This content is for general information only. It does not, and is not intended to, provide legal or technical advice or to represent a legal interpretation of the matters it addresses.

## The law on seatbelts in tractors

Under current road traffic law, you do not have to wear a seat belt on a tractor, even where one is fitted. However, we would always advise the use of a seat belt for your own safety.

By law you are not required to have a seatbelt fitted to your tractor however if fitting one, we recommend that it is fitted by a suitably qualified individual (SQI) in line with manufacturer’s specifications and meets the essential technical provisions of[Regulation (EU) No 167/2013.Open in new browser tab](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/PDF/?uri=CELEX:32013R0167&rid=2)
