# Vehicle standards for buses and minibuses.

Road Users
### Types of buses and your obligations to maintain safety standards as a bus operator.

Buses are vehicles which are designed and constructed for the carriage of more than eight passengers in addition to the driver.

This content is for general information only. It does not, and is not intended to, provide legal or technical advice or to represent a legal interpretation of the matters it addresses.

* * *

## There are two categories of bus

![Image 1: mini-bus](https://www.rsa.ie/images/default-source/cta-card-(450-x-298)/mini-bus.jpg?sfvrsn=5b486e1a_0)

##### M2 - Bus

A small bus which can carry more than eight passengers in addition to the driver and has a maximum mass **not** exceeding five tonnes.

![Image 2: mini-bus](https://www.rsa.ie/images/default-source/cta-card-(450-x-298)/bus25d3f2ac980c42afbd4e1c4c14cb12b4.jpg?sfvrsn=a1dfb0b7_0)

##### M3 - Bus

A large bus which can carry more than eight passengers in addition to the driver and has a maximum mass exceeding five tonnes.

## Maintaining and repairing your bus

As the owner or operator of a bus, it's very important to always keep your vehicle in a roadworthy condition.

All bus operators must ensure buses are roadworthy. They must:

*   Have in place regular and good quality [preventative maintenance systems](https://www.cvrt.ie/en-US/Operator-Driver-Obligations/maintenance-and-repairs/)
*   Conduct [daily walkaround checks](https://www.cvrt.ie/en-US/Operator-Driver-Obligations/Daily-walk-around-checks-and-defect-detection/)
*   Swiftly [repair defects](https://www.cvrt.ie/en-US/Operator-Driver-Obligations/maintenance-and-repairs/)
*   Ensure [buses are tested on time](https://www.cvrt.ie/en-US/Test-Bookings/)​
*   Complete [an annual self-declaration](https://www.cvrt.ie/en-US/Operator-Driver-Obligations/Self-declaration/?_gl=1*1sljb0l*_ga*MTg4NzI4MTIxNC4xNjk1NzI5OTY3*_ga_J53N6LXN3P*MTcwMTM2MTAwNy4xMTYuMS4xNzAxMzYyODU0LjAuMC4w) with the Road Safety Authority.

Aside from the road safety benefits and it being your legal obligation, well maintained buses reduce the likelihood of your bus encountering delays from unscheduled downtime, unsafe buses being impounded and additional delays at roadside inspections.

You can find out more on the [CVRT vehicle maintenance](https://www.cvrt.ie/en-US/Operator-Driver-Obligations/maintenance-and-repairs/)and repairs website.

Driving for work involves a risk not only for the driver, but also for fellow workers and members of the public, such as pedestrians and other road users.
