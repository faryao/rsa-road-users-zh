# Reflective markings on heavy commercial vehicles

### Marking requirements for heavy commercial vehicles.

This content is for general information only. It does not, and is not intended to, provide legal or technical advice or to represent a legal interpretation of the matters it addresses.

* * *

## Frequently asked questions

[What vehicles must be fitted with reflective markings?](http://www.rsa.ie/road-safety/road-users/professional-drivers/vehicle-safety-legislation/heavy-commercial-vehicles/reflective-markings-on-heavy-commercial-vehicles#)

Reflective or high visibility or Conspicuity markings are required on the following vehicles and trailers or combinations:

[Are rear and side markings required on motor caravans' motorhomes, buses, ambulances or fire engines?](http://www.rsa.ie/road-safety/road-users/professional-drivers/vehicle-safety-legislation/heavy-commercial-vehicles/reflective-markings-on-heavy-commercial-vehicles#)

No.

[What are the standards or specifications for reflective markings?](http://www.rsa.ie/road-safety/road-users/professional-drivers/vehicle-safety-legislation/heavy-commercial-vehicles/reflective-markings-on-heavy-commercial-vehicles#)

Either Irish markings or UN ECE Regulation 104 markings are acceptable in Ireland.

Tape used for retro-reflective marking to UN ECE 104 standard must be type approved and carry E markings at least every 50 cm.

Refer to the[HCV Manual Open in new browser tab Open in new browser tab](https://www.cvrt.ie/en-US/About-CVRT/New-CVR-Testing-Manuals/)for further detail.

[What is the difference between side reflectors and side markers or lamps?](http://www.rsa.ie/road-safety/road-users/professional-drivers/vehicle-safety-legislation/heavy-commercial-vehicles/reflective-markings-on-heavy-commercial-vehicles#)

Side reflectors are strips of material which run along the side of the vehicle.

Side markers are amber lamps which are fitted to the side of a vehicle or trailer every 3 m. or less.

[Do vehicles or vans used for road and motorway maintenance need reflective markings or chevrons?](http://www.rsa.ie/road-safety/road-users/professional-drivers/vehicle-safety-legislation/heavy-commercial-vehicles/reflective-markings-on-heavy-commercial-vehicles#)

Yes, if they exceed 3,500 kg or form part of a combination of vehicles exceeding 11 m. overall in length. However, if you are being contracted to do work for a local authority or agency, they may require reflective markings or chevrons to be fitted to your vehicle(s) as part of the job. In this situation you would need to contact them directly.

Our[Guidance for retroreflective marking on LCVs](http://www.rsa.ie/docs/default-source/road-safety/r1.5-professional-drivers/guidance-for-retroflective-marking-on-lcvs.pdf?sfvrsn=d399afb_5)document provides information for owners of LCVs that would like to fit retroreflective chevron markings to their vehicle. Further information can be found on our [Standards for light vehicles](http://www.rsa.ie/road-safety/road-users/professional-drivers/vehicle-safety-legislation/light-commercial-vehicles) webpage.
